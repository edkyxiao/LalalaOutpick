Lalala Outpick resumed our litter auditing again at Masons Fall, Kinglake National Park in March. 12 outpickers collected more than 106 pieces of litter, weighing more than 2 kg. The majority of them are food wraps, cig butts and other soft plastic.

Nestled within the majestic Kinglake National Park, Masons Falls offers a serene escape into nature’s embrace. As we embarked on litter-picking through lush forests and scenic trails, the soothing sound of cascading water may indicate the tail of the summer. And we look forward to litter-picking in the autumn!
