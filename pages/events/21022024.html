About Events

Step into the mystique of Hanging Rock with a unique event combining the iconic Australian New Wave film ‘Picnic at Hanging Rock’ and a community litter-picking effort. For the February event, Lalala Outpick enjoyed a half day of cinematic wonder and environmental stewardship as we explored the breathtaking landscape at Hanging Rock. The beauty and sensation of the history and nature are still lingering around.
