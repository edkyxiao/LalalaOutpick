May's litter-picking event along Merri Creek in Brunswick was our second time there. The inner suburb waterway is flowing water and its reflection under the sun. As we walked the trail, we noticed that things have changed since our first time here back in April 2022 - the unfortunate presence of litter, from plastic bottles, snack wrappers, to soft ball and condom. The plastic bags still hang on the trees. 13 outpickers from our community joined this litter-picking event and let's continue to protect and cherish our urban environment.
