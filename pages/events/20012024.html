Experiencing the power of expression as queer women come together for a queer feminist stand up and collage workshop. In the beginning of 2024, Lalala Outpick tried something different - from outdoor litter picking to the indoor activities, we organised one stand up (5th Jan) and a collage workshop (3rd Feb).

Verbal and visual expression is the way to liberate our creativity. We explored identity and activism through expressing yourself and doing art. And we also shared experience and laughter, and even celebrated our voices, stories and solidarity.
