May 2023, Lake Wendouree in Ballarat

On a rainy and gloomy day, Lalala Outpick headed to Ballarat for our litter-picking in May. Located in central Ballarat next to the Botanical Gardens, Lake Wendouree offers a wide range of attractions and recreational activities both on and off the water. It was a beautiful destination for all the outpickers: willows, eucalyptus, swans, ducks, birds and wetland, the urban lake offers a diverse ecosystem. It was an easy walk without a heavy duty of litter-picking. However, beer bottles and cans are still the most-picked objects (and interestingly we found a used condom!?). We did a semi-circle walk around the lake and ended up with a cosy brunch at a cafe, then called it a day!
