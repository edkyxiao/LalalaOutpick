September 2023, Mt Lofty - Wonga Park

On a sunny shining day, outpickers headed to Mt Lofty Wonga Park for our litter-picking in September. Surprisingly, spring came earlier than we expected and it was a pretty warm day. The land was beautifully fresh and vivid green while Yarra River meandered gently nearby, reflecting the azure sky above. The trail was generally clean, giving a more relaxing vibe for our walk. As the trail ascended gradually, offering panoramic vistas of the Yarra Valley below.
