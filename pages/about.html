ABOUT US

We are Lalala Outpick, a queer women-led environmental group established in 2022.
We strive to provide a safe place for queer women and allies from diverse backgrounds,
connecting with each other by litter picking around Victoria.

Our Story

——

Litter picking is the most achievable and accessible thing we can do for our environment.
We regard our beautiful landscape and value the unique Australian flora and fauna.

We want to connect and encourage queer women and women-identifying allies from
diverse backgrounds to see the environment where we are, appreciate the beauty of
nature, learn environmental conservation, socialise with like-minded people, to find a
sense of belonging within the community by litter picking.

Lalala OutPick is a not-for-profit organisation. We facilitate monthly litter picking events
around Victoria. We encourage people to join us.

Our People

——

Jing Yang | co-lead

Jing is a people enthusiast and a budding trash queen.
She has a vision to transform the language of waste
into valuable materials, transform the linear system
which is currently 'take-make-dispose' into a circular
economy, a closed loop system of things. She believes
waste is purely human invention and there is no such
thing as waste in nature, everything has its value.

Lesley Le Luo | co-lead

Lesley is a PhD candidate and uni tutor in digital media
and screen culture. She is trying to figure out the use of
digital media in relation to queer women's
representation and identity formation. Lesley believes
that human mobility is the reason we waste our
environment, but also the solution to save it. She is also
an emerging filmmaker and occasionally makes short
films focusing on queer women representation.

Liz Li | wildness first aider

Liz is a landscape architect and qualified wildness first
aid responder. Liz spends her 9-5 in the office, tries to
work out a Solarpunk future for the urbanized mother
earth, then gets out and proud to the blue and green
outside working hours. Liz is passionate about plants,
rocks, and waves. Start your conversation with her
from those items in the Lalala walk, you will get a great
chat :).
