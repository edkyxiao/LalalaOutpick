June 2023, Outpicker Screening on Bourke Street

A Special night for a special screening. Outpicker is the short documentary created by our two co-leads Lesley and Jing, and other creatives. We invited more than 60 people for the premiere of Outpicker this winter night. Outpicker tells the story of Jing, a young queer migrant woman who finds the sense of belonging through litter picking and community building across Victoria. Jing narrates herself and Lesley is the director of this short doco (what a great duo!). Simply, Outpicker is an environmental poem that is dedicated to all our outpickers and the stunning Victorian environment!
