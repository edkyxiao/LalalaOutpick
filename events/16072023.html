July 2023, Westgate Park

As the winter passed quickly, Lalala Outpick organised the July event in an urban park just down the Westgate Bridge. 11 outpickers joined us in the urban landscape of Westgate Park. As a green oasis in the heart of industrial Melbourne, it is popular with cyclists, joggers and walkers. But also as expected, the park's cleanliness was compromised, with large bottles scattered along the trail and small plastic objects demanding attention at every turn. After the litter picking, outpickers headed to the nearby South Melbourne Market for a meal and some good chats.
