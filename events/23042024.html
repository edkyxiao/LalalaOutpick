About Events

32 people participated in this special joint event with Yellow Kitties. We walked five kilometres, picking up various types of litter from the creek within the estuary to Mt Martha Beach and collecting over 10kg of litter. The area is rich in biodiversity and offers 5 kilometres of walking boardwalks, and we witnessed the plantation and seabirds.
